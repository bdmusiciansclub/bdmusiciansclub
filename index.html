About Us

Bangladesh Musician's Club · Est. 2022

"We Are The Soul Of Music"

Bangladesh Musician's Club (BMC) was established in 2022 with a vision to bring all music industry professionals under one unified platform in Bangladesh. We believe every successful event is a collective achievement — from the artist on stage to the crew working tirelessly behind it.

From artists and sound engineers to lighting designers, LED operators, and production managers — BMC ensures every professional is recognized and valued equally across all three sectors.

Our Goals

  • ♦Build a strong professional network across the music industry
  • ♦Support skill development for emerging professionals
  • ♦Help qualified professionals find career and gig opportunities
  • ♦Establish dignity and recognition for music arts and culture
  • ♦Ensure fair recognition for all three sectors of the industry
  • ♦Grow into a national organization representing musicians across Bangladesh
