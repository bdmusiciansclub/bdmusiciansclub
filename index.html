About Us

Bangladesh Musician's Club · Est. 2022

"Where Music Becomes Brotherhood"

Bangladesh Musician's Club (BMC) was established in 2022 with a vision to bring all music industry professionals under one unified platform. We believe every successful event is a collective achievement — from the artist on stage to the crew working tirelessly behind it.

From artists and sound engineers to lighting designers, LED operators, and production managers — BMC ensures that every professional is recognized and valued equally.

Our Goals

  • ✦ Build a strong professional network among music industry workers
  • ✦ Support skill development for emerging professionals
  • ✦ Help qualified professionals find career opportunities
  • ✦ Establish dignity and recognition for music arts and culture
  • ✦ Ensure fair recognition for all four sectors of the industry
